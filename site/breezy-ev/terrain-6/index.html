Common questions

What people ask about the Terrain 6.

How fast does the Terrain 6 go?

The Terrain 6 is programmable up to 25 mph, which is the maximum allowed for a Low Speed Vehicle (LSV) on Texas streets. We program every cart at delivery to match how you plan to drive it — slower for kid-driving, faster for property use.

What's the range on a single charge?

Breezy EV rates the Terrain 6 at 35–50 miles per charge on the standard 48V 125Ah Lithium pack. Real-world range depends on terrain, payload, and speed — Lake Livingston customers typically get a full weekend of cruising without needing to plug in.

Is it street legal in Texas?

Yes — with the optional street-legal kit the Terrain 6 is registered as a Low Speed Vehicle, which Texas allows on roads posted 35 mph or less. The kit adds headlights, turn signals, mirrors, seat belts, a horn, and a license-plate mount. We handle the install and the paperwork.

What's the warranty?

Every new Breezy EV comes with a 2-year bumper-to-bumper warranty on the cart and an 8-year warranty on the Lithium battery pack. PCGC backs the warranty locally — call us, we pick the cart up, we fix it, we bring it back.

Is the Terrain 6 good for off-road and ranch use?

Yes — the Terrain 6 ships lifted from the factory with 7.1 inches of ground clearance and 23x10-14 DOT-rated off-road tires. It's the right fit for gravel roads, pasture, and the trail to the dock or the deer blind.
