Brand new Breezy Ev Golf Carts

Our Mission

At Polk County Golf Carts, our mission is to provide top-quality brand new or used golf carts to our customers!


There's No better ride than a Breezy Ev, Style. Comfort. Speed. Fun!

Come live the Breezy Life with Polk County Golf Carts!

Bluetooth

These all new Breezy EV Come with the sound bars with LED light and Bluetooth. As well as a Touch Screen that has Apple & Android CarPlay!

technology

The Controller in this cart has the capability to lock down the cart via the app. Also if you have children driving the cart you can turn it down to 5 Mph and lock it out!


When the parents drive it has the capability to speed up to 37 Mph or slower, your choice!

Specs on the carts

These carts come with a 2 Year Bumper to Bumper warranty with a 8 year warranty on the Lithium battery!

Full Street Legal kit

Available in Four Seaters or Six all seats facing the same direction!
